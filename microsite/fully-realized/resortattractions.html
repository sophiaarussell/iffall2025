Attractions

Siren's Curse
❮
❯

North America's tallest, longest, and fastest tilt coaster which reaches a maximum height of 160 feet, with a top speed of 58 mph.

Steel Vengeance
❮
❯

A record-breaking 205-foot hybrid coaster delivering nearly 30 seconds of airtime—the most in the world—with four inversions and speeds up to 74 mph.

Maverick
❮
❯

An intense launched coaster featuring a 95-degree drop, a twisted horseshoe roll, and two launches up to 70 mph through unpredictable terrain-hugging turns.

Rougaru
❮
❯

Cedar Point's floorless coaster that sends riders with dangling feet through a 137-foot drop and multiple inversions at 60 mph.

Cork Screw
❮
❯

A historic 1976 steel coaster that was the world's first to feature three inversions, with its iconic blue track looping above the park midway.

Sky Hawk
❮
❯

A giant swing ride that launches riders up to 125 feet in the air at 60 mph using massive pneumatic-powered arms.
